// THE PROBLEM

A tale of two classrooms.

India's NEP recognises experiential learning as essential — yet the reality is deeply unequal. IIT students have world-class labs. Crores of students copy experiment write-ups for experiments they will never perform.

A chemistry student who has never mixed reagents cannot build intuition. Memorising facts without doing is not education.

// IIT DELHI — TIER 1
World-class labs, simulation software, research mentorship. Graduates with genuine practical intuition.
// GOVT. COLLEGE, RURAL BIHAR
A textbook, a blackboard, a lab last functional 5 years ago. Pass exams but cannot solve real problems.
// VIRTULAB — THE BRIDGE
Immersive virtual labs. Zero cost. Works on any phone. Builds genuine intuition through doing, not reading.
